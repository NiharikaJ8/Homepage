Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most beautiful city in the world. It has many tourist places like Eiffel tower, Louvre Museum etc. Eifffel tower is one of the seven wonders in the world. It's the most beautiful tourist attraction in Paris. It's the most visited place all over the world. Louvre Museum is a palace, which once was the home for France's kings. It has a glass pyramid which the visitors can view while entering the courtyard of the palace. It also has the most famous painting the Mona Lisa, which was painted by Leonardo da Vinci.
